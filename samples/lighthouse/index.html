Light House

This demo was one of the first I made to test the export format of the engine. As such, the shading is the simplest possible, and there are no textures (they weren't supported at the time)

The options let you play with the camera's configuration and the mouse sensitivity.

Controls

  • left click: rotate the camera around the focus point.
  • middle click: move the focus point in a screen aligned plane.
  • right click: zoom in / out.

Sources

The model is the Lighthouse (challenge #16) by Carlos Silva
